There are 6 types of Meta-operators that each enhance the behavior of the regular operators.
Some of these operators can also be found in Perl 5 and in other languages. Others are more
special to Perl 6.
Assignment Operators
Negated Relation Operators
Reversed operators
Hyper operators
Reduction operators
and
Cross operators

See also <a href="http://perlcabal.org/syn/S03.html#Meta_operators">S03</a>
